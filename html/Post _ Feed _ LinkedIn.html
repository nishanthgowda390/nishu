"Learning is endless, Knowledge is limitless. There is always more room for learning every day."

Few weeks back I completed my online remote based internship at Entuple Technologies as Design Engineering Intern and attained my certificate. Everyday was great learning experience and had the opportunity to work on creating System on Chip (SoC) designs by implementing Register transfer level (RTL) coding for CPU micro-architecture using System Verilog and UVM. Also, I had the privilege to work on FPGA design for low pass FIR filter (DSP architecture) and implemented failure analysis during testing process. The complete design methodology was performed using EDA tools like SimVision, ModelSim and scripting languages like C/C++ and python.

Overall, it was an incredible experience and I am looking forward to apply my knowledge and skills in future.

Anyone out there who has an interest in Digital design and verification I’d love to connect and happy to help by sharing my resources and experience which can be mutually beneficial.
